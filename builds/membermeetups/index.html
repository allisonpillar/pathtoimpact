Member Meetups: Your Pathway to Even Greater Results

May 22-23, 2018 | Minot, Bismarck, Fargo, ND
Share
Tweet

Meet members and hear what 2018 has in store with at this year’s membership orientation sessions. This session is designed for you to explore the details of your member benefits, ask questions, connect with peers, and help shape programming for the year. 

These sessions are split into two parts: the first part is a broad overview designed for new and veteran members, and the second part is a detailed overview designed primarily for new members or those with technical questions.

 

Minot – Register

Tuesday, May 22 | 1:30-3:30pm

Broadway Bean and Bagel Co., 1701 South Broadway, Minot, ND 58701

 

Dickinson – Register

Thursday, May 24 | 9-11am

The Brew, 215 Sims Street, Dickinson, ND 58601

 

Fargo – Register

Tuesday, June 12 | 3-5p

Dakota Medical Foundation, 4141 28th Avenue South, Fargo, ND 58104

 

Bismarck – Register

Thursday, June 14 | 10am-12pm

Bismarck-Mandan Chamber of Commerce, 1640 Burnt Boat Drive, Bismarck, ND 58503

 

Grand Forks – Register

Thursday, June 21 | 10am-12pm

Choice Health & Fitness, 4401 South 11th Street, Grand Forks, ND 58201

 

By the end of these sessions, you will:

  • Have a full understanding of the benefits of Impact Institute membership
    • Know the key dates of trainings for this year
    • Understand the history of Giving Hearts Day and the benefits of participation
    • Know the details and capabilities of your impactgiveback.org giving page
  • Learn the details and benefits of taking the 2018 Fundraising Assessment 
    • Understand when and how to take the assessment, and how the report will be used to improve your organization’s fundraising
    • Know how results will be used in RevUp groups to develop a plan
    • Q&A time to answer any questions you still have about being an Impact Institute member!
